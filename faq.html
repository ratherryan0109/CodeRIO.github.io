General

CodeRio is an interactive coding education platform designed to help developers learn programming through hands-on courses, practice challenges, real-world projects, and a supportive community. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, CodeRio provides structured learning paths, coding exercises, and mentorship opportunities to accelerate your growth.
Yes! CodeRio offers a generous free tier that includes access to introductory courses, practice challenges, community forums, and basic progress tracking. Premium subscriptions unlock advanced courses, unlimited practice, mentorship, offline access, and priority support. You can upgrade at any time from your account settings.
CodeRio is designed for learners of all levels: complete beginners starting their coding journey, students supplementing their computer science education, professionals looking to upskill or switch careers, and experienced developers wanting to stay current with new technologies. Our content ranges from HTML/CSS basics to advanced system design and AI/ML topics.
CodeRio combines structured courses with hands-on practice and real projects in a single platform. Our unique features include adaptive learning paths, a built-in typing lab for coding speed, peer collaboration on projects, mentorship programs, and gamification that makes learning engaging. We focus on practical, job-ready skills rather than just theory.

Learning

Learning paths are curated sequences of courses and challenges designed to take you from beginner to advanced in a specific domain. Each path is split into milestones with clear learning objectives. You can follow a path sequentially or jump to specific topics based on your existing knowledge. Your progress is saved automatically as you complete each step.
Absolutely! You can enroll in multiple learning paths simultaneously and switch between them at any time. Your progress is tracked independently for each path. We recommend focusing on one path at a time for best results, but you're free to explore different topics as your interests evolve.
Each course is divided into modules, which contain multiple lessons. Lessons include video content or readings, interactive code examples, and coding exercises. Modules end with quizzes, and courses culminate in a final project. You earn XP and badges as you complete each component.
Yes! Upon completing a course or learning path, you receive a certificate of completion that can be shared on LinkedIn or added to your resume. Premium subscribers receive verified certificates with unique credentials that employers can validate.
We recommend at least 30 minutes of focused practice daily for optimal progress. Our learning paths are designed with this in mind, with lessons typically taking 15-45 minutes to complete. Consistency matters more than duration, so even short daily sessions lead to significant progress over time.

Technical

CodeRio supports Python, JavaScript, TypeScript, Java, C++, C#, Go, Rust, Ruby, PHP, Swift, Kotlin, and SQL. Our code runner and interactive editor work with all supported languages. We regularly add new languages based on community demand.
No installation is required for most features. Our in-browser code editor lets you write, run, and debug code directly in your browser. For project-based courses that require local development, we provide setup guides for your preferred operating system.
CodeRio works best on the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience. The platform is fully responsive and works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
Our code runner executes your code in secure, isolated containers on our servers. It supports standard input/output and includes common libraries and frameworks. Execution time is limited to ensure fair usage, with generous limits for most exercises. The runner provides detailed error messages to help you debug.
Yes! Your code is automatically saved as you type in our editor. You can also manually save versions of your code. All your submissions and solutions are stored in your account and accessible from your code history dashboard.

Account

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner. You can register with your email address or use Google/GitHub OAuth for quick sign-up. Verify your email to activate your account. The entire process takes less than 2 minutes.
Click "Log In" and then select "Forgot Password." Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. The link expires after 1 hour for security reasons. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder.
Yes. Go to your account settings and select "Delete Account." You'll be asked to confirm and provide a reason for leaving. After confirmation, your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. Note that this action cannot be undone.
Subscriptions are billed monthly or annually. Premium plans unlock unlimited practice, advanced courses, mentorship, offline access, and priority support. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your account settings. Annual plans offer a discount compared to monthly billing.
Yes. Navigate to your profile settings and update your email address. You'll need to verify the new email before the change takes effect. Your account data and progress remain intact during the change.

Dashboard

Your dashboard tracks courses completed, challenges solved, projects built, XP earned, streak days, and skill proficiency across different domains. Each course shows lesson-by-lesson completion status. Progress data is updated in real-time as you learn.
XP (Experience Points) are earned by completing lessons, challenges, and projects. They contribute to your level and leaderboard ranking. Streaks track consecutive days you've completed at least one learning activity. Longer streaks unlock special badges and bonus XP multipliers.
Your skill radar chart is displayed on your dashboard. It visualizes your proficiency across categories like algorithms, data structures, web development, databases, and system design. The chart updates as you complete relevant exercises and assessments.
Yes! Your dashboard includes optional leaderboard features. You can compare XP, streaks, and challenge completion rates with friends or the global community. Leaderboard participation is opt-in, and you can set your privacy preferences in account settings.
Badges are awarded for specific accomplishments: completing your first course, solving 100 challenges, maintaining a 30-day streak, helping other users in forums, and more. Badges appear on your profile and dashboard. Collecting all badges in a category unlocks a special trophy.
