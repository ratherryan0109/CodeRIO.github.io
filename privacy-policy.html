Privacy Policy

Learn how we collect, use, and protect your personal data. Last updated: June 1, 2026.

Data Collection

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve our coding education platform. Transparency about what we collect and why is a core part of our commitment to you.

We collect:

  • Email address — used for account verification, notifications, and password recovery
  • Full name — displayed on your public profile and used for personalization
  • Course progress — tracks your lesson completion, quiz scores, and project submissions
  • Typing statistics — WPM, accuracy, and consistency data to power your typing lab dashboard
  • Project submissions — code you write and submit as part of coursework and challenges

We do NOT collect:

  • Payment information — all payments are processed securely by Stripe; we never see your card details
  • Plaintext passwords — your password is hashed using bcrypt before it ever reaches our servers
  • Precise location data — we only infer a general region from your IP address for analytics

Cookies

CodeRio uses cookies to enhance your experience, maintain session integrity, and improve our platform. Here is what each category of cookie does:

  • Essential cookies — required for authentication, session management, and platform security. These cannot be disabled without affecting site functionality.
  • Preference cookies — remember your theme selection (light/dark), language preferences, and UI settings across sessions.
  • Analytics cookies — optional cookies that help us understand how you use the platform so we can improve content and features.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings at any time. Disabling essential cookies may prevent parts of the platform from functioning correctly.

Security Practices

Protecting your data is our highest priority. We implement industry-standard security measures across every layer of our infrastructure:

  • AES-256 encryption at rest — all user data stored in our databases is encrypted using the Advanced Encryption Standard with 256-bit keys
  • TLS 1.3 in transit — every connection between your browser and our servers is secured with the latest TLS protocol
  • Regular security audits — our codebase and infrastructure undergo automated vulnerability scanning and third-party penetration testing
  • No plaintext passwords — passwords are hashed with bcrypt (salt rounds: 12) before storage; we never have access to your original password
  • Session management — all sessions expire after 24 hours of inactivity and tokens are rotated regularly to prevent session hijacking

User Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy regulations, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access your data — request a complete copy of all personal data we hold about you at any time
  • Delete your account — permanently delete your account and associated data through your profile settings
  • Export your data — download all your data in a portable format (JSON/CSV) for transfer to another service
  • Opt out of analytics — disable analytics cookies and prevent your usage data from being collected
  • Update personal info — edit your name, email, and profile details directly from your account settings

To exercise any of these rights, visit your profile settings or contact our privacy team directly.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Our retention schedule is designed to balance platform functionality with your privacy rights:

  • Active accounts — your data is retained for as long as your account remains active and in good standing
  • Deleted accounts — upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently purged within 30 days
  • Backups — encrypted backups containing user data are retained for a maximum of 90 days before being overwritten
  • Anonymized analytics — aggregated, anonymized usage statistics may be retained indefinitely for platform improvement

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, want to exercise your data rights, or need to report a security concern, please reach out to our privacy team:

  • Email: privacy@coderio.dev
  • Response time: We respond to all privacy inquiries within 48 hours
  • DPO: Our Data Protection Officer can be reached directly at dpo@coderio.dev for sensitive matters

For urgent security vulnerabilities, please use our responsible disclosure process by emailing security@coderio.dev with the subject line "Security Report".
